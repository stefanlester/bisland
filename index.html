🌟 Who Are We?

#ChangeMakers #BislandCares

Bisland Charity Foundation is a non-profit organization based in Ghana, dedicated to improving lives through sustainable projects and community events.

✅ Successfully completed water project in Kodie-Apagya (2023), providing access to clean and safe water to over 5,000 people.
⚽ Organized charity football events bringing together local teams to raise awareness and foster unity.
🏫 Donated construction materials to Dwamena Akenten Senior High School in Offinso, supporting 43 students.
🏃‍♂️ Hosted community sports events promoting youth engagement and healthy lifestyles.
