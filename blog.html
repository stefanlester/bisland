Kodie-Apagya Water Project: Transforming Lives Through Clean Water Access

June 29, 2023 | BCF Team

After months of planning and community collaboration, we're thrilled to share the successful completion of the Kodie-Apagya Water Project. This transformative initiative now provides clean, safe drinking water to over 500 residents in the community.

The project included drilling a new borehole, installing modern pumping equipment, and creating a sustainable water distribution system. Thanks to our generous supporters and dedicated volunteers, families no longer need to walk long distances for clean water.

Water Project Sod Cutting Ceremony Marks New Beginning

March 29, 2023 | Community Relations

The official sod cutting ceremony for the Kodie-Apagya Water Project marked the beginning of a transformative journey for the community. Local leaders, volunteers, and residents gathered to witness this historic moment.

The ceremony featured traditional blessings, community speeches, and the symbolic breaking of ground. Children were especially excited to see the start of their new water facility.

"Today marks the beginning of a new chapter for our community. Clean water means health, education, and hope for our children's future." - Traditional Leader
