Kodie-Apagya Water Project: Bringing Clean Water to 5,000 Residents

June 15, 2025 | Bisland Team | 24 Comments

After months of planning and community collaboration, we're thrilled to announce the successful completion of the Kodie-Apagya Water Project. This transformative initiative now provides clean, safe drinking water to over 5,000 residents in the community.

The project included drilling a new borehole, installing modern pumping equipment, and creating a sustainable water distribution system. Thanks to our generous donors and dedicated volunteers, families no longer need to walk long distances for clean water.

100 Bags of Cement Donated to Dwamena Akenten Secondary School

July 2, 2025 | Bisland Foundation | 8 Comments

In our continued commitment to education infrastructure, Bisland Charity Foundation generously contributed 100 bags of cement to Dwamena Akenten Secondary School to support their ongoing construction projects.

100 Bags of Premium Cement
Value: ₵15,000

This donation will help the school complete their new classroom block, providing better learning environments for over 800 students. The headmaster and school community expressed heartfelt gratitude for this timely support.

"Education is the foundation of progress, and we're proud to support infrastructure that will benefit generations of students," said our project coordinator.
