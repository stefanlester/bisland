Our Story & Mission

Building hope, transforming lives across Ghana

Founded on Compassion

The Bisland Charity Foundation was born from a simple yet powerful belief: every community deserves access to clean water, education, and opportunities to thrive.

Since our inception, we have been dedicated to creating sustainable change across Ghana, focusing on water projects, educational support, and community development initiatives that address the real needs of our people.

Our work spans from rural villages to urban communities, always with the same commitment: empowering locals to build a better future for themselves and their children.

Our Impact Today

Through strategic partnerships and community-driven initiatives, BCF has successfully implemented life-changing projects across multiple regions of Ghana. From providing clean water access to over 5,000 people in Kodie-Apagya to organizing charity football events that unite communities, we continue to inspire change.

Our recent milestone includes supporting Dwamena Akenten Secondary School with 100 bags of cement, demonstrating our commitment to educational infrastructure development.
